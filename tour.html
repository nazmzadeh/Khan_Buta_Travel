Information about tour

Located in the picturesque city of Fethiye, in the south of Turkey, "Liberty Hotel" is an ideal place to relax. This modern resort offers the highest level of comfort and luxury with stunning sea and mountain views.

"Liberty Hotel" offers its guests many facilities and services. Sea or mountain view rooms are also among your options.

You can enjoy different tastes from Turkish cuisine to international cuisine with the wide selection offered at the hotel's restaurants and bars.

These hotels are suitable for lovers of both active and passive recreation. For those who prefer active rest, there is a wide range of fun-filled activities from the swimming pool to the gym, from the SPA to the water park.

Fethiye, where the sea meets the mountains and creates magnificent views, will leave you unforgettable memories. Liberty hotels located here are ideal options to experience Fethiye in the most beautiful and luxurious way.
